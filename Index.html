How to read this

  • Figures are derived only from what your spreadsheet contains. Where data is missing, exposure is understated — not absent.
  • Replacement values use purchase cost plus your uplift. They are an indication for planning, not a valuation.
  • Useful life defaults to 15 years where a row does not specify one.
  • Duplicate detection ignores case, spacing and punctuation, so AHU-01 and ahu 01 are treated as the same asset.
